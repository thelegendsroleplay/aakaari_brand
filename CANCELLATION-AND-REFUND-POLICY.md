## 1. Our Policy

### 1.1 Core Policy

Herrenn operates under a **7-DAY REPLACEMENT ONLY** policy:
- ✅ **YES:** We offer replacements for defective or wrong products
- ❌ **NO:** We do not provide refunds
- ❌ **NO:** We do not allow order cancellations after payment

### 1.2 Why Replacement Only?

We offer replacements instead of refunds to:
- Ensure you receive the quality product you ordered
- Maintain our commitment to customer satisfaction
- Stand behind the quality of our products
- Provide a fair resolution for genuine issues

### 1.3 Timeline

Replacement requests must be made within **7 days of delivery**.

## 4. 7-Day Replacement Policy

### 4.1 Replacement Window

You have **7 days from the date of delivery** to request a replacement.

**Example:**
- Order delivered: January 1st
- Last day to request replacement: January 7th
- After January 7th: No replacements accepted

### 4.2 Counting Days

- Days are counted from the date of delivery (not order date)
- Includes weekends and public holidays
- Based on delivery date shown in tracking

### 4.3 What is Covered

Replacements are provided for:
- ✅ Defective products
- ✅ Damaged products
- ✅ Wrong product sent
- ✅ Manufacturing defects
- ✅ Missing parts or accessories
- ✅ Size discrepancy (if product is wrongly sized)

## 5. Eligibility for Replacement

### 5.1 Eligible Reasons

Replacements are accepted if:

**Defective Products:**
- Stitching defects
- Fabric defects
- Print defects
- Zipper/button issues
- Manufacturing flaws

**Damaged Products:**
- Product received in damaged condition
- Torn or stained fabric
- Damaged packaging with product damage
- Visible damage during delivery

**Wrong Product:**
- Different product than ordered
- Wrong size delivered
- Wrong color delivered
- Missing items from order

### 5.2 Documentation Required

You must provide:
- Clear photos of the product
- Photos showing the defect/damage
- Photos of product tags and packaging
- Order number
- Delivery invoice

### 5.3 Initial Inspection

Our team will review your request and:
- Verify the issue
- Confirm eligibility
- Approve or reject the request
- Provide response within 24-48 hours

## 6. Non-Replaceable Items

### 6.1 Items That CANNOT Be Replaced

The following are **NOT eligible for replacement**:

**Used or Worn Products:**
- Products that have been worn
- Products with signs of use
- Washed products
- Products with altered tags

**Missing Components:**
- Products without original tags
- Products without brand labels
- Missing packaging materials
- Missing invoice/bill

**Personal Preference:**
- Don't like the fit (unless size is wrong)
- Don't like the color (unless color is wrong)
- Don't match expectations
- Change of mind

**Hygiene Items:**
- Innerwear and undergarments
- Intimate apparel
- Socks and stockings
- Face masks

**Special Items:**
- Final sale items
- Clearance products marked "non-returnable"
- Customized or personalized products
- Products on special promotions (if specified)

**Damage Due to Misuse:**
- Customer negligence
- Improper care or washing
- Accidental damage after delivery
- Pet damage or stains

**Late Requests:**
- Requests made after 7 days of delivery

## 11. Quality Check

### 11.1 Inspection Process

When we receive your return, we:

**Step 1: Physical Inspection**
- Check overall product condition
- Verify tags and packaging
- Check for signs of use

**Step 2: Defect Verification**
- Verify the reported defect/damage
- Compare with photos provided
- Check if it's a genuine issue

**Step 3: Eligibility Confirmation**
- Confirm product meets return conditions
- Verify it's within the 7-day period
- Check if it's a replaceable item

**Step 4: Decision**
- Approve replacement
- Or reject replacement (with reason)

### 11.2 Inspection Timeline

Quality check takes **2-3 business days** after receiving the product.

### 11.3 Discrepancies

If we find discrepancies:
- Photos don't match actual product
- Issue is not as described
- Product has been used
- Tags are missing

**We will:**
- Contact you for clarification
- May reject the replacement
- Return the product to you
