## 1. Shipping Coverage

### 1.1 Domestic Shipping

We currently ship to all serviceable locations within **India only**.

**Serviceable Areas:**

We are continuously working to expand our delivery network to reach more areas across India.

- **Expected Delivery Date**
- **Tracking Link**

### 5.2 How to Track

**Method 2: Our Website**
- Login to your account
- Go to "My Orders"

### 5.3 Tracking Updates

- Allow 24 hours after dispatch

## 6. Delivery Process

### 6.1 Delivery Attempt

Our courier partner will:
- Call you before delivery
- Deliver to the address provided
- Request signature/OTP for verification
- Hand over the package

### 6.2 Delivery Verification

**For UPI/Prepaid Orders:**
- OTP-based delivery (if available)
- Signature confirmation
- ID proof may be required

**For Cash on Delivery:**
- Payment collected at delivery
- Exact amount preferred
- Digital payment options available with courier

### 6.3 Delivery Timing

Deliveries are attempted:
- **Monday to Saturday:** 10 AM - 7 PM
- **Sunday:** No deliveries (most locations)
- **Public Holidays:** No deliveries

### 6.4 Package Inspection

**You have the right to:**
- Check package condition before accepting
- Refuse damaged packages
- Open and verify contents (some couriers allow)

**If Package is Damaged:**
- Don't accept the delivery
- Take photos/videos immediately
- Contact us within 24 hours
- We'll arrange replacement

### 6.5 Contactless Delivery

For safety and convenience:
- Packages can be left at your doorstep
- Photo confirmation may be taken
- OTP or signature still required
- Specify preference during checkout

## 7. Delivery Attempts

### 7.1 First Attempt

Courier will attempt delivery on the estimated date.

**If You're Not Available:**
- Courier will call/SMS you
- Leave a delivery note
- Attempt next delivery

### 7.2 Second Attempt

Next business day delivery attempt.

**What You Should Do:**
- Be available at given address
- Keep phone accessible
- Inform someone at home

### 7.3 Third Attempt

Final delivery attempt.

**If Missed:**
- Package may be sent to courier office
- You'll need to pick up
- Or package returned to us

### 7.4 Failed Delivery

After 3 failed attempts:
- Package returned to our warehouse
- You'll be notified via email/SMS
- Refund initiated (minus shipping charges, if applicable)

**To Avoid Failed Deliveries:**
- Provide accurate address
- Include landmark
- Keep phone number active
- Inform family/neighbors
- Specify alternate delivery time (if available)

## 9. Shipping Delays

### 9.1 Possible Delay Reasons

Delays may occur due to:
- **Weather Conditions:** Heavy rain, storms, floods
- **Natural Disasters:** Earthquakes, cyclones
- **Political Situations:** Strikes, protests, bandhs
- **Festival Rush:** High order volumes during festivals
- **Remote Locations:** Difficult accessibility
- **Courier Issues:** Vehicle breakdown, staff shortage
- **COVID-19 or Health Emergencies:** Restrictions, lockdowns
- **Incorrect Address:** Undeliverable addresses
- **Custom Clearance:** For rare cases

### 9.2 Communication During Delays

If there's a delay:
- We'll notify you via email/SMS
- Updated delivery timeline provided
- Tracking status updated
- Support team available for queries

### 9.3 What We Do

During delays, we:
- Monitor shipments closely
- Coordinate with courier partners
- Keep you informed
- Escalate if needed
- Expedite delivery when possible

### 9.4 What You Can Do

If delivery is delayed:
- Check tracking status
- Contact courier partner
- Contact our support team
- Be patient during force majeure events

## 10. Lost or Damaged Shipments

### 10.1 Lost Shipments

If package is lost in transit:

**Our Resolution:**
1. Investigation with courier partner
2. Wait period: 7-10 business days
3. If confirmed lost: Reship product
4. If out of stock: Refund provided

**Your Action:**
- Contact us immediately
- Provide order and tracking details
- Cooperate with investigation

### 10.2 Damaged During Transit

If package arrives damaged:

**Immediate Action:**
1. Don't accept the delivery (if visible damage)
2. Take photos/videos
3. Note damage on courier receipt
4. Contact us within 24 hours

**Our Resolution:**
- Free replacement arranged
- Pickup of damaged item
- No questions asked

### 10.3 Partial Delivery

If some items are missing:

**Report Within:**
- 24 hours of delivery

**Our Action:**
- Verify order details
- Dispatch missing items
- Or provide partial refund

### 10.4 Wrong Product Delivered

If you receive wrong product:

**Our Resolution:**
- Free pickup arranged
- Correct product dispatched
- Expedited delivery
