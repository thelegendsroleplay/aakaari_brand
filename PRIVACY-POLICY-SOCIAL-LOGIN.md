## Social Login & Third-Party Authentication

### What We Collect Through Social Login

When you choose to sign in or register using a social login option (Google, Facebook, etc.), we collect limited information from your social media account to create and manage your account on our site.

**Information collected may include:**
- Full name
- Email address
- Profile picture
- User ID from the social platform

### How We Use Social Login Data

We use the information collected through social login services to:
- Create and maintain your account
- Authenticate your identity
- Personalize your shopping experience
- Communicate with you about orders and account updates
- Comply with legal obligations

### Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party authentication services:

#### Google Sign-In
When you use Google Sign-In, you authorize us to access certain information from your Google account according to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We only request access to basic profile information (name, email address, and profile picture).

**Google's Privacy Policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

#### Facebook Login
When you use Facebook Login, you authorize us to access certain information from your Facebook account according to Facebook's Terms of Service and Data Policy. We only request access to basic profile information (name, email address, and profile picture).

**Facebook's Data Policy:** https://www.facebook.com/privacy/explanation

### Data Storage and Security

- Social login data is stored securely in our database
- We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for data transmission
- We do not store your social media passwords
- We do not have access to your social media account credentials
- Profile information is only used for account creation and authentication

### Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- Disconnect social login at any time from your account settings
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Update your profile information
- Access your personal data
- Withdraw consent for data processing

To disconnect a social login provider or delete your data, please contact us at [your-email@domain.com] or visit your account settings page.

### Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your social login information with third parties except:
- When required by law
- To protect our rights and safety
- With service providers who help us operate our website (under strict confidentiality agreements)

### Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

### Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 through social login or otherwise.

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

### Contact Us

If you have questions about how we handle your social login data, please contact us:
- Email: [your-email@domain.com]
- Phone: [your-phone-number]
- Address: [your-business-address]

## GDPR Compliance (For EU Users)

### Legal Basis for Processing
We process your data under the following legal bases:
- **Consent:** You provide explicit consent when choosing to use social login
- **Contract:** Processing is necessary to provide our services
- **Legitimate Interest:** To prevent fraud and maintain security

### Your GDPR Rights
If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights:
- Right to access your data
- Right to rectification
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at [your-email@domain.com]

### Data Transfers
When you use social login, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside the EEA. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in accordance with GDPR requirements.
